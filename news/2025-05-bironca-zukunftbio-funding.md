---
title: _BiRonCa_ bioreactor project funded by ZukunftBio.NRW
date: 2025-05
tag: funding
summary: Funding · ~€850,000 over two years for bubble-free bioreactor scale-up with Biothrust GmbH
---

The state of North Rhine-Westphalia is funding the "Bionic Reactors for
Cardiac Organoids (BiRonCa)" project for two years with around €850,000.
Together with Biothrust GmbH (Aachen), the lab is testing a hollow-fibre
gas transfer system that lets cardiac-organoid bioreactors run at higher
cell densities without bubble formation, scaling up from 200 mL to
2–3 L reactor volumes.
