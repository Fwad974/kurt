---
title: Official opening of the _Marga and Walter Boll Laboratory_
date: 2023-06-22
tag: milestone
summary: Milestone · Inauguration event at the Medical Faculty, University of Cologne
---

On 22 June 2023 the Marga and Walter Boll Laboratory for Cardiac
Tissue Engineering was officially opened at the large lecture hall of
the physiology department of the Medical Faculty, University of
Cologne. Invited speakers included Prof. Konrad Brockmeier, Prof.
Bastiaan Boukens (Maastricht), Prof. Horst Fischer (RWTH Aachen),
Dr. Nadine Nottrodt (Fraunhofer ILT), Katharina Schwab, Philipp Jahn
and Lisa Münchhalfen.
