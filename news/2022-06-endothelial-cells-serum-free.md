"High-efficient serum-free differentiation of endothelial cells from
human iPS cells."

Hamad S, Derichsweiler D, Gaspar JA, Brockmeier K, Hescheler J,
Sachinidis A, Pfannkuche KP. _Stem Cell Research & Therapy_, 2022 Jun
11; 13(1):251.

The protocol generates hiPSC-derived endothelial cells at
90.9 ± 1.5% efficiency in only six days, without animal-derived
serum, and transfers cleanly from 2D monolayers into 3D bioreactor
suspension cultures.
