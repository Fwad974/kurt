What we collect

When you write to us by email we receive your email address, your name if provided, and the content of your message. No cookies, analytics trackers, or additional personal data are collected or processed through this website beyond technically necessary server logs.

How data is used & deleted

Your data is used exclusively to handle and reply to your enquiry. It is not passed to third parties for any other purpose. We adhere to the principle of data minimisation — your data is stored only as long as necessary and deleted routinely once the enquiry has been resolved.

You may request deletion at any time by writing to kurt.pfannkuche@uni-koeln.de. Any previously given consent may be withdrawn at any time with effect for the future.

Your rights under GDPR

You may exercise any of the following rights at any time by contacting the controller above:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure of your stored data
  • Restriction of processing where legal obligations prevent immediate deletion
  • Objection to processing of your data
  • Data portability where processing is based on consent or contract

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority. A directory of national authorities is available at www.bfdi.bund.de →
