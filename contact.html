§ 02 — Enquiry types

What we respond to.

We try to respond to all relevant enquiries within five working days. The more specific your message, the quicker we can help.

Collaborations

Academic & industry partnerships

Proposals for collaborative projects, material transfer, or joint grant applications. Include a brief description of your group and the intended scope.

Positions

PhD, postdoc & student enquiries

Unsolicited applications are welcome. Please include your CV, a brief motivation letter, and an indication of your preferred start date.

Press & media

Interviews & media enquiries

Journalists and science communicators may contact the PI directly. High-resolution microscopy images and figures are available on request for editorial use.

GDPR

Data protection notice

Privacy · GDPR-compliant personal data handling for all enquiries

Personal data submitted via email enquiry is processed solely for the purpose of responding to your message, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the data protection policies of the University of Cologne.

Data is not shared with third parties and is not retained beyond the duration required to handle your enquiry. You may request deletion at any time by writing to kurt.pfannkuche@uni-koeln.de.

What we collect. When you contact us by email, we receive your email address, name (if provided), and the content of your message. No other personal data is collected through this website.

Your rights. Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data we hold about you. You may also object to processing or request restriction. To exercise any of these rights, contact the PI directly.
